Center Object Super massive Blackhole

The Milky Way is a massive barred spiral galaxy containing 100–400 billion stars, including our Sun, spanning approximately 100,000 to 200,000 light-years across. It features a central bar, a luminous bulge, and distinct spiral arms where we reside, surrounded by a dark matter halo. Viewed from Earth, it appears as a bright band across the sky
