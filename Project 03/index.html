Natural Disasters

A natural disaster is a major adverse event resulting from natural processes, such as floods, hurricanes, earthquakes, and wildfires. These events cause significant destruction, loss of life, and economic damage, occurring hundreds of times yearly. Common types include geological events (earthquakes, volcanoes) and weather-driven events (cyclones, droughts)
